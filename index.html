Visit Santa Maria (RS - Brazil)

Enjoy culture, nature and music in this little town in the south of Brazil

Top three activities to do at Santa Maria

Federal University Of Santa Maria (UFSM)

Federal University of Santa Maria was the first federal university created in the interior, outside of a Brazilian capital. This fact represented an important milestone in the process of internalizing public university education in Brazil and contributed to Rio Grande do Sul becoming the first State in the Federation to have two federal universities.

Treze de Maio Theater

Theatro Treze de Maio is an old theater in the Brazilian city of Santa Maria, in the state of Rio Grande do Sul, located in Saldanha Marinho square, in the city downtown. It is a property listed by the municipality.

Gorgeous nature sites

Santa Maria has several locations with exuberant nature, such as waterfalls, forests, lakes and hills
